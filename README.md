# Cross-border Market Entry Strategist
Systematic market selection and entry strategy for cross-border e-commerce expansion.
## What it does
- Evaluates multiple international markets using a multi-factor scoring model (purchasing power, logistics, regulatory, competition)
- Generates phased entry roadmaps based on timeline and budget, recommending marketplace-first vs. DTC approaches per market
- Produces implementation checklists covering VAT, compliance, logistics, payments, and market-specific requirements
## Example scenarios
**1. 潮汕牛肉丸想进新加坡市场**
> 👤 我在汕头做牛肉丸的小厂，想把产品卖到新加坡去，华人多应该好做，第一步怎么走？
> 🤖 新加坡市场进入策略：1）合规先行——新加坡食品局（SFA）要求肉类制品需有Halal认证（即使华人顾客，超市渠道也要求）；2）产品形态——推荐真空冷藏包装，保质期从现做的3-5天延长至冷冻30天；3）渠道——先通过Shopee Singapore/Lazada跨境卖家入驻试水，再谈线下超市代理；4）定价——双倍国内售价（运输+关税+渠道成本）。建议先参加新加坡食品展FHA招代理。

**2. 评估五个欧洲市场**
> 👤 "Score these 5 European markets for our premium pet food brand's first international launch."
> 🤖 Weighted scoring model — market size, pet ownership rates, premium willingness-to-pay, e-commerce penetration, regulatory ease, competitive density — with top-2 recommendation and entry sequencing.

**3. 东南亚市场进入模式选型**
> 👤 "We want to enter Southeast Asia. Market entry mode: marketplace-first or DTC site?"
> 🤖 Mode comparison for each country (Singapore→DTC, Indonesia→Shopee/Lazada-first, Thailand→hybrid) with logistics, brand control, and customer acquisition cost trade-offs.
